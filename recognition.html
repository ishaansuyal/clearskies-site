Honors

ClearSkies has been recognized at both the state and national level for its contribution to environmental science and community health.

National Nomination  ·  Environmental Category

Gloria Barron Prize for Young Heroes

ClearSkies received a nomination for the Gloria Barron Prize for Young Heroes in the environmental category, a national prize that honors young people who have made a significant positive difference to people and the planet.

SSTFI 2025  ·  Engineering Category

2nd Place: State Science & Technology Fair of Iowa

At the State Science and Technology Fair of Iowa (SSTFI), ClearSkies placed 2nd in the Engineering category, competing against projects from across the state and earning recognition for its rigorous hardware and data methodology.
